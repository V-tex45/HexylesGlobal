About Hexyles Global

Building excellence since 2025

Our History

Founded in 2025, Hexyles Global has began as a small construction firm with a big vision. we aspire to grown into one of Kenya's most respected construction companies, completing projects across Kenya and East Africa.

Our commitment to quality, safety, and innovation will earn us numerous awards and the trust of both private and public sector clients.

Our Vision

To be Africa's leading construction company, recognized for excellence in building sustainable and innovative structures.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional construction services through skilled craftsmanship, cutting-edge technology, and unwavering commitment to client satisfaction.

Our Values

Integrity, Quality, Innovation, Safety, and Sustainability guide every project we undertake.
